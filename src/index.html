    What We're All About

    Our shelter is home to dozens of dogs, cats, and other animals that have been abandoned or lost by their original owners. It is our mission to provide each of them with food, shelter, protection, and comfort, but we need your help. Whether it is through adoption, donations, volunteer work, or pet reunification efforts, one thing is for sure—these wonderful critters all rely on your help and support.

    • Across the United States, about 6.3 million companion animals enter animal shelters each year.
    • Of these animals, approximately 920,000 are euthanized every year.
    • As of 2023, many Utah shelters are overflowing, with little or no space to spare for new arrivals.
    • When you adopt from an animal shelter, you save two lives—the pet you adopt, and the one who will take their open space in the shelter.
