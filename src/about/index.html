About Us

    Who We Are

    Precious Paws Animal Shelter was founded in 1995 by Doctor Noah Williams, a local veterinarian who wanted to make a difference. Now, the shelter is home to almost one hundred animals who would otherwise be left on the street. Our staff and volunteers are dedicated to providing each one of these animals with food, water, shelter, and stimulation, and it is our goal to help as many of them as possible to find loving homes of their own.

    Our Mission

    Our aim is to make our community a safer, happier space for the animals that live here by providing humane care and adoption for as many of them as possible. In addition to this, we also aim to reduce the number of strays on the streets by providing and advocating for better education for pet owners and especially those who are thinking about purchasing a new pet.

    Our Values

    We offer warm and compassionate care for all animals in our keeping.

    We save and sustain life where it is possible, and offer humane euthanasia to alleviate suffering when it is not.

    We seek to serve and benefit our community.

    We work with transparency and integrity.

    We are inclusive and respecful in to both our employees and the public.
